## Requirements

- 6 or 8 WLED-compatible LED strips.
- WLED board supporting 6 or 8 GPIO pins (i.e. ESP32).
- Sufficient power supply and any other WLED requirements.
- 3D printed parts from one of the `models` subfolders.
